Privacy Policy — Stone Worth AI

Last updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Stone Worth AI (a WorthScan AI app) collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. Stone Worth AI provides educational field-check guidance only and is not a certified appraisal or certified identification service.

Information we collect

  • Account email — used to create and manage your account.
  • Uploaded photos — the photos you submit for an educational scan.
  • Scan answers and item details — the simple questions you answer about an item.
  • Saved finds — items you choose to save in the app.
  • App usage — information needed to operate your account, credits, and features.

How your photos are used

Photos are used to provide app functionality and educational scan analysis. Please do not upload personal or sensitive photos that are unrelated to checking an item.

Service providers

  • Supabase — used for authentication, database, storage, and backend services.
  • AI service providers — used to perform educational scan analysis on submitted photos and details.
  • Google Play services — may apply if you installed the app through Google Play.

Your choices and controls

  • You can delete saved finds in the app.
  • You can delete your account and associated data in the app where available.
  • You can contact us for privacy questions or data deletion requests at the email below.

Educational use

Stone Worth AI is educational only. It is not a certified appraisal or certified identification. See our Disclaimer for details.

Contact

For privacy questions or account/data deletion requests, contact: stoneworthai.support@gmail.com
