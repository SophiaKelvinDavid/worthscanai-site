Stone Worth AI

An educational field check for stones, minerals, crystals, and polished specimens.

Stone Worth AI helps users perform an educational field check of stones, minerals, crystals, and polished specimens.

Users can upload one or two photos, answer simple item questions, and receive cautious AI-generated guidance including:

  • Possible candidates
  • Info match
  • Worth-checking score
  • Value / rarity guidance
  • Suggested next steps

How it works

  • Upload one or two photos of your item.
  • Answer a few simple questions about the item.
  • Receive cautious, educational AI-generated guidance.

Stone Worth AI is educational only. It is not a certified appraisal, certified mineral identification, laboratory report, or market valuation service.
