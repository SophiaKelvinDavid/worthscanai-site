Refund Policy — Flip Worth AI

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This page describes the refund, purchase, and cancellation policy for Flip Worth AI (a WorthScan AI app).

Purchases through the App Store

Flip Worth AI offers optional purchases: the Starter Monthly subscription (30 scans/month, $4.99/month), the Plus Monthly subscription (100 scans/month, $9.99/month), and the one-time Scan Pack 20 (+20 scans, $2.99). All subscriptions and scan packs are processed through the Apple App Store.

Refunds

Refunds are handled by Apple according to App Store policy. To request a refund, contact Apple Support or visit your Apple account purchase history. We do not process direct refunds for purchases made through the App Store.

Cancellations

Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing period unless cancelled beforehand in your Apple account settings.

Questions

If you have questions or need help, contact us at: flipworthai.support@gmail.com
