Refund / Purchases / Cancellation Policy — Flip Worth AI

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This page describes the refund, purchase, and cancellation policy for Flip Worth AI (a WorthScan AI app).

Purchases through Apple App Store or Google Play

If Flip Worth AI offers paid plans, subscriptions, scan credits, or in-app purchases, all purchases made through the Apple App Store or Google Play are subject to the refund and cancellation policies of those stores. We do not process direct refunds for purchases made through Apple or Google.

  • Apple App Store: Contact Apple Support or visit your Apple account purchase history to request a refund.
  • Google Play: Contact Google Play Support or visit your Google Play order history to request a refund.

Cancellations

Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time through your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. We are not responsible for managing subscriptions purchased through those platforms.

Questions

If you have questions about a purchase or need help, contact us at: flipworthai.support@gmail.com
