Privacy Policy — Flip Worth AI

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Flip Worth AI (a WorthScan AI app) collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. Flip Worth AI provides educational field-check guidance for secondhand, thrift, garage sale, estate sale, and used items. It is not a certified appraisal or authentication service.

Information we collect

  • Account email — used to create and manage your account, and for login.
  • Uploaded item photos — the photos you submit for an educational scan.
  • Scan results — the AI-generated educational guidance returned for each scan.
  • Saved finds — items you choose to save in the app.
  • Scan credits and app usage data — information needed to operate your account, enforce usage limits, and maintain security.
  • Support messages — if you contact us for help, we retain the content of those communications.

How your information is used

  • Login and account management.
  • AI scan analysis on submitted photos and item details.
  • Saving and displaying your results within the app.
  • App functionality and feature access.
  • Usage limits and scan credit tracking.
  • Security and fraud prevention.
  • Responding to support requests.

How your photos are used

Photos are used to provide app functionality and educational scan analysis. Please do not upload personal or sensitive photos that are unrelated to checking an item.

We do not sell personal data

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Service providers

We may use third-party service providers to operate the app, including for authentication, database and storage, AI scan analysis, email delivery, and app distribution. These providers are given access only to the information needed to perform those services.

Your choices and data deletion

  • You can delete saved finds within the app.
  • You can delete your account and associated data. See our Delete Account & Data page for instructions.
  • You can contact us for privacy questions or data deletion requests at the email below.

Educational use

Flip Worth AI is educational only. It is not a certified appraisal or authentication service. See our Disclaimer for details.

Contact

For privacy questions or account/data deletion requests, contact: flipworthai.support@gmail.com
